Leadership

Julie Cooper, PH.D.

President and CEO Julie Cooper has served as president and chief executive officer of Power College since July 2014. As president at PC, she successfully launched and met the College’s $20 million campaign gained PC acceptance into the AACC Pathways Project funded by the Bill and Melinda Gates Foundation. A collaborative partnership with local universities. In 2021, under her leadership, PC won the American Association of Community College’s (AACC) Award of Excellence in both the Student Success category, as well as Safety and Planning. Dr. Cooper earned a Doctor of Philosophy in Education Research and Evaluation from BCU, a Master of Science in Organizational Communication from EU, and a Bachelor of Arts in Political Science from MSU.
