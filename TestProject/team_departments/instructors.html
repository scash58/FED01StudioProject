Justine Binford

Justine Binford is an associate professor of English literature at the University of Nevada, Reno. Her teaching and research focus on American and African American literature. He specializes in popular literature, archival research, and critical theory.

Professor Data

Data is a self-aware, sapient, sentient and anatomically fully functional male android who serves as the second officer and chief operations officer. His positronic brain allows him impressive computational capabilities. He experienced ongoing difficulties during the early years of his life with understanding various aspects of human behaviour[3] and was unable to feel emotion or understand certain human idiosyncrasies, inspiring him to strive for his own humanity.
